Meat And Fish

Cats require animal protein to thrive, and meat and fish are excellent sources of essential amino acids, vitamins, and minerals.

Cooked Eggs

Certain Vgetables

Some vegetables, like carrots, peas, and spinach, can provide vitamins and minerals, but they should be fed in small amounts and only occasionally as cats don't digest them well.

Natural Yogurt

Can provide calcium and probiotics, but it should be plain and unsweetened.

Hard Cheese

Can provide calcium and probiotics, but it should be plain and unsweetened.
